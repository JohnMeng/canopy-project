Want open standards and free software for the Internet of Things?

Developing a cloud-managed product today is an expensive and challenging endevour. We want to change that. Software infrastructure for smart products should be freely available and easy to use.

Today's consumers expect a seamless experience from their smart devices, with beautiful UIUX, watertight security, and flawless performance. Canopy helps device makers exceed these expectations. As an end-to-end solution with a modular design, device makers can leverage as much or as little of the platform as they need.
