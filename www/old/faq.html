What is Canopy?
Canopy is an Internet of Things software platform, similar to Wink or Arrayant Connected Platform. Canopy lets you remotely control, monitor, and share physical devices, from anywhere, using your smartphone or web browser.

Unlike other IoT platforms, Canopy is completely open source and royalty free. Canopy provides a standardized format for describing the capabilities of a smart product. Canopy transforms this description into software objects that can easily be used by application developers.

Why is Canopy open source?
Today, the Internet of Things is dominated by proprietary platforms. We think it should be more like the World Wide Web: based on royalty-free standards and open source software.

Most of the appliances in our homes and workplaces will soon be connected to the Internet. Many companies want to control the underlying IoT infrastructure with proprietary software. That way, they can charge expensive licensing and service fees and profit from your data.

At Canopy, we have a different mindset. We want to foster an open IoT ecosystem that encourages interoperability and freedom.

We belive a product's owner should be able to choose whose "cloud" it is talking to, or even run a Canopy cloud server from home if she chooses.

We believe it should be free and simple for manufacturers to make their products "smart" with Canopy, without us even knowing.

We believe application developers should be able to interact with physical devices using standardized interfaces and libraries.

Philosophy aside, there are many practical benefits to open source model. Making the software free and publicly-available encourages adoption. Also, the code benefits from community contributions and bug fixes.

How is Canopy funded?
Currently, Canopy is self-funded by the lead developer. If you know of any sponsorship or funding opportunities, please let us know!

How will Canopy make money?
The Canopy team will host the "official" deployment of the Canopy Cloud Service. It will be free for devices to connect and free for users to join. We will offer premium service to users for a small monthly fee ($1/device/month).

Is Canopy secure?
Security is perhaps the biggest concern people have about IoT. Needless to say, we take it seriously.

In our opinion, the best way to make IoT secure is to leverage the security mechanisms and best practices that make the web secure. The technology that keeps my online banking transactions secure can be used to prevent hackers from opening my garage door or starting my dishwasher.

We are working on documenting a comprehensive threat model and commited.
