Monthly pricing is based on a baseline fee per device, plus a combined storage fee, plus a combined data transfer fee.

Fee Cost (Yearly) Cost (Monthly)
Baseline Fee $0.75 per device $0.0625 per device
Data Storage Fee $0.12 per MB $0.01 per MB
 
Fee Cost
Data Transfer Fee $0.01 per Mbit

For example, if you have 10,000 devices that use a combined 3GB of storage, and transfer a combined 15 Gbit of data each month, the total yearly fee will be:

10000×$0.75 + 3000×$0.12 + 15000×12×$0.01 = $9360.00

Which is ~$0.93/device/year or ~$0.08/device/month.
