Internet of Things

Monitor.  Control.  Share.

Canopy is an open source IoT software platform that is exceptionally easy to use and develop for.


Beautiful Control.  From Anywhere.

Canopy-enabled devices can be controlled from the web or your phone.
At home or on the go.

Each device has a unique interface that manufacturers can customize to match the look-and-feel of the real thing.

Designed for Sensors

Our platform is designed to store massive amounts of sensor data in the cloud, and to present this data with stunning visuals.

Bring on the big data.

Built-in Social Network of Things

Share your things with others. Leave notes and comment on things.

With Canopy's social features, you can let the delivery man access your garage door for the next 15 minutes. Or notify your housemates that the smart coffee machine needs more filters.

Best-in-class developer tools and APIs.

"class fan" : { "control speed" : { "min-value": 0, "max-value": 4, }, "sensor temp" : { "datatype": "float32", "units": "degrees_c" } }
 Developing a smart product has never been easier. Just describe the product using SDDL (Smart Device Description Language), and our embedded software tool generates most of the code and UI for you.

App developers can use our REST API or client libraries to interact with Canopy-enabled devices from their own applications.

Completely Open Source

It's simple, really. We believe that the Internet of Things, like the World Wide Web, should be based on open software and royalty-free standards.

That's why we've made all of our source code available under the highly-permissive Apache License v2.0, and all of our specifications and documentation available under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License.

Free hosting. Worry-free cloud.

We take care of the cloud, so you don't have to even think about it.

Our hosted platform is free. Devices connect for free. End-users manage their devices for free. We plan to eventually cover our costs through ad revenue and premium features.
