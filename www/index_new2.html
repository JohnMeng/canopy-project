Monitor.  Control.  Share.
Canopy is an open source end-to-end IoT platform that is exceptionally easy to use and develop for.


Beautiful Control.  From Anywhere.

Canopy-enabled devices can be controlled from the web or your phone.

Each device has a unique interface that manufacturers can configure to match the look-and-feel of the real thing.

Need to make your product smart? Just describe your product in our
Smart Device Description Language, and our embedded software tool generates most of the code for you.

App developers can use our REST API or client libraries to interact with Canopy-enabled devices from third-party applications.
