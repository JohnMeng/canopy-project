Internet of Things
made simple.
Monitor.  Control.  Share.
Canopy is an open source end-to-end IoT platform that is exceptionally easy to use and develop for.


Beautiful Control.  From Anywhere.

Canopy-enabled devices can be controlled from the web or your phone.
At home or on the go.

Each device has a unique interface that manufacturers can configure to match the look-and-feel of the real thing.


Designed for Sensors

Our platform is designed to store massive amounts of sensor data in the cloud, and to present this data with stunning visuals.

Bring on the big data.


Built-in Social Network of Things

Share your things with others. Let the delivery man access your garage door for the next 15 minutes.

Leave notes and comment on things. Notify your housemates that the smart coffee machine needs more filters when they next open the app.


Best-in-class developer tools and APIs.

"class fan" : { "control speed" : { "min-value": 0, "max-value": 4, }, "sensor temp" : { "datatype": "float32", "units": "degrees_c" } }
Developing a smart product has never been easier. Just describe the product using our Smart Device Description Language, and our embedded software tool generates most of the code and UI for you.

App developers can use our REST API or client libraries to interact with Canopy-enabled devices from third-party applications.


Hosted for free.

We take care of the cloud, so you don't have to.

Users can control up to 5 devices on our hosted platform for free. After that, it's just $1.00/device/month, charged directly to the end-user.
