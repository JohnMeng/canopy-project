Brief History of Canopy

Greg Prisament started Canopy as an open source project in April 2014. Previously, he had worked as a software architect at PowerCloud Systems (acquired by Comcast) and Ubiquiti Networks.

These roles introduced Greg to the awesomeness of cloud-managed devices. The products he worked on (such as the SkyDog Router) could be monitored and controlled over the web using apps. But he also experienced first-hand the vast complexity and difficulty of bringing "smart" products to market. He knew there needed to be a better way.

Greg started Canopy with a clear goal: to simplify the development of cloud-enabled products.

After reading a book on the origins of the World Wide Web, Greg was inspired by how open source software and royalty-free protocols enabled the web to take off. As such, Greg committed early on to building open source software and royalty-free protocols for IoT.
